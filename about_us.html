About Us

Amir Construction and Co. has started its journey 10 years ago. It has its head office in Dhaka, Bangladesh. The company deals with different equipment like Excavator, Crane, Bulldozer, Dump Truck, Wheel Loader and Container. Amir Construction and Co., has business connection with Chinese and Russian Companies. Amir Construction and Co., has a good number of equipment stocks, simply saying, it is one of the top 3 companies in the territory dealing with mentioned items. There are different building blocksbased on which the company is breathing over others, like, superior service, proper documentation, on time notification and commitment.
